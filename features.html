Modules

Three modules. Built to plug in.

Each Valentia module ships standalone or together. Same data layer, same UI, same auth. Pick what your team needs — extend over time.

Operations

Run protocols, not chaos.

Every property management operation runs on routines: turnovers, owner onboarding, maintenance, inspections. Valentia Operations turns those into versioned protocols your team actually follows — with auto-assignment, SLAs, and audit trails.

  • Protocol library with versioning and rollback
  • Owner module: structured onboarding and recurring touchpoints
  • Cleaning & maintenance ops: scheduling, dispatch, photo proof
  • SLA tracking and automated escalations
  • Mobile-friendly checklists for field staff
Marketing

Owner acquisition on autopilot.

Most property managers grow by referrals — slow and unpredictable. Valentia Marketing builds a repeatable pipeline: targeted owner outreach, LinkedIn automation, lead capture, and nurture flows that actually convert.

  • LinkedIn outreach automation with personalization
  • Landing pages and lead capture forms (no-code)
  • Email and WhatsApp nurture sequences
  • Owner CRM with deal stages and source tracking
  • Attribution back to closed contracts
Finance

Owner statements that don't require spreadsheets.

Property management finance is messy by default: split fees, reimbursements, owner deductions, multi-currency. Valentia Finance handles the reconciliation so your monthly close is hours, not days.

  • Automated owner statements with branded PDFs
  • Fee structures: management %, fixed, hybrid, per-channel
  • Reservation-level revenue and cost tracking
  • Reimbursements and owner deductions handled cleanly
  • Export to QuickBooks, Xero, or your accountant's format
